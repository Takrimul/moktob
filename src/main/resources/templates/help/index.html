Student Management

  1. Navigate to the Students section from the sidebar
  2. Click the "Add Student" button
  3. Fill in the required information:
    • Student's full name
    • Guardian's name and contact information
    • Class assignment
    • Date of birth
  4. Click "Save" to add the student

  1. Go to the Students section
  2. Find the student you want to edit
  3. Click the "Edit" button next to their name
  4. Update the information as needed
  5. Click "Save Changes"
Teacher Management

  1. Navigate to the Teachers section
  2. Click the "Add Teacher" button
  3. Enter teacher details:
    • Full name and contact information
    • Qualifications and certifications
    • Subjects they can teach
    • Class assignments
  4. Save the teacher profile
Attendance Management

  1. Go to the Attendance section
  2. Select the class and date
  3. Mark students as present or absent
  4. Add any notes if necessary
  5. Save the attendance record
Reports & Analytics

  1. Navigate to the Reports section
  2. Choose the type of report you need:
    • Student attendance reports
    • Class performance summaries
    • Teacher workload reports
    • Financial reports
  3. Select date ranges and filters
  4. Click "Generate Report"
  5. Export or print the report as needed
Frequently Asked Questions

To reset your password:

  1. Click on your profile in the top-right corner
  2. Select "Settings" from the dropdown
  3. Go to the "Security" tab
  4. Click "Change Password"
  5. Enter your current password and new password
  6. Click "Update Password"

  • Go to the Students section
  • Click the "Export" button
  • Choose your preferred format (Excel, CSV, PDF)
  • Select the data fields you want to include
  • Click "Download"
